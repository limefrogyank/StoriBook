Intro to Chemistry & the Scientific Method

Learning Objectives

In this module, our goals are to:

  1. Develop an understanding of chemistry.
  2. Define the steps of the scientific method.
  3. Recognize the difference between a scientific theory and scientific law.
  4. Distinguish between qualitative and quantitative observations.
  5. Classify independent, dependent, and controlled variables

Example of Variables in an experiment

Will a cube of sugar dissolve faster in hot water?

Independent Variable

Temperature of the water

Dependent Variable

Time it takes for sugar cube to dissolve

Controlled Variable(s)

Size/shape of sugar cube, amount of water, size of container, level of agitation (stirring)
