Guidebook 4A: What Are Earthquakes?

Earthquakes scare people … a lot! That’s not surprising because time and time again earthquakes have caused massive damage and many, many casualties. Anyone who has lived through a damaging earthquake cannot forget the experience . But geoscientists and engineers are getting better at understanding earthquakes, minimizing the amount of damage they cause, and reducing the number of people affected.
